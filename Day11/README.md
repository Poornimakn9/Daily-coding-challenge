**Day 11: Insurance & Claim Incident Analysis (Power BI)**
** Objective**

Analyze insurance claims data to identify patterns in claims, detect fraud indicators, and evaluate relationships between customer demographics, policy details, and claim amounts.

** Dataset**

The dataset includes:

Policy details (Annual Premium, Deductible, Umbrella Limit)
Customer demographics (Age, Occupation, Education)
Incident details (Type, Severity, Location, Date)
Claim data (Total Claim Amount, Property/Vehicle/Injury Claims)
Fraud status (Fraud Reported)
** Tools & Skills Used**
Power BI
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Data Modeling
DAX (Measures & Calculated Columns)
Interactive Dashboard Design

** Key Insights**
 Fraud Analysis
Around 25% of total claims are fraudulent
Fraud is more common in specific incident types (e.g., single/multi-vehicle incidents)
 Regional Trends
Certain states show higher claim frequency, indicating risk concentration
 Customer Insights
Age group 25–40 has the highest number of claims
Certain occupations contribute significantly to claim amounts
 Financial Insights
Weak correlation between premium and claim amount
High claim values exist even for lower premium policies
 Trend Analysis
Claim amounts show slight fluctuations over time
No consistent upward or downward trend
